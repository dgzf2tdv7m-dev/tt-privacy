PanYard TT Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-15

App Overview

PanYard TT is an offline iOS utility for organizing pan yard records, checklists, notes, and reference details on the device.

This policy applies to the iOS app identified by bundle ID com.panyard.tt.

Data Collection

PanYard TT does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share any personal data. App content is intended to remain on the user's device and is not sent to our servers.

  • No account registration or sign-in.
  • No analytics, tracking, advertising, or profiling.
  • No cloud synchronization operated by the app.
  • No third-party SDKs for analytics, ads, attribution, or crash reporting.
  • No payment processing or external user database.

Local Storage

Any notes, lists, records, preferences, reminders, or reference selections created in PanYard TT are stored locally on the device using Apple's standard app storage. Removing the app may remove this local information. If the user enables optional system features such as local notifications, those permissions are handled by iOS and can be changed in Settings.

Children, Tracking, and Advertising

PanYard TT is designed as a private, offline utility. It does not knowingly collect information from children, does not display personalized advertising, and does not use identifiers to track users across apps or websites.

User Control and Data Rights

Because the app does not operate an account service or remote database, we do not have access to view, export, correct, or delete user-created app content. Users control their local data directly on their device. They can edit or remove records inside the app where those controls are provided, manage permissions in iOS Settings, or delete the app to remove its local container from the device.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published on this page with a new revision date. The app's privacy position is intentionally simple: no account, no tracking, no advertising, no cloud sync, no third-party analytics SDK, and no collection of personal data by the developer.
